Good vision leads to a good future.

Many schoolchildren are deeply impacted by living with blurry vision, restricting the ability to learn visually. In fact, 80% of learning happens visually. Children are the face of the future, and if we don't allow each and every student to reach their potential, the lack of resources will result in our future to suffer.

Adults suffer from vision impairment as well, its effects seen in everyday activities such as driving, and is ultimately a great risk for others if not treated.
