Take Action!

Below are some resources, links, and ideas to help get the issue of eyecare in low-income communities around!

  • Lion's Club:
     An organization that strives to improve the quality of life for the low-income and visually impaired.


  • Respectacle:
     A non-profit organization that recycles used glasses to those who need it.


  • Giving Sight:
     This site delivers professionals, needed eyecare, and infrastructure, their goal being universal access for eyecare to those in need.


  • Start your own Organization:
     Collect glasses in your community and get in touch with other organizations near you who have the same mission as you!
